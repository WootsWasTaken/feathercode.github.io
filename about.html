DESIGN AND SIMPLICITY

At FeatherCode we focus at simplicity and design, making the product simple and easy to use. Every day, we work on a better user experience and the products. We love feedback and privacy, we never ask for more information than necessary.

All of our products are built around the same few things: simplicity, speed, privacy and design. If it's cool then we'll probably make it, hardware or software. That's why at FeatherCode we simplify everything.
